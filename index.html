BTC Neural Net Predictor

  • BiLSTM + multi-head Attention trained on 500K+ BTC/USDT 1-hour candles from Binance — OHLCV, volume profile, funding rate
  • Solved the cold-start problem: model bootstraps from pretrained weights then fine-tunes live on a rolling window, so it adapts to new market regimes without full retraining
  • Custom asymmetric loss penalises false positives harder than misses — aligns gradient descent directly with PnL, not just prediction accuracy
  • WebSocket inference pipeline feeds live ticks → preprocessor → model → signal in <50ms end-to-end
  • Kelly-criterion position sizing + automated stop-loss; backtested Sharpe of 1.82 over 18-month out-of-sample window

WorldPulse

  • Streams live headlines from GNews, Guardian, and NewsAPI — 50+ sources deduplicated with cosine similarity before NLP processing

  • FastAPI backend with WebSocket broadcasting — client receives classified articles within 800ms of publication
  • Redis cache for deduplication; Celery workers for parallel NLP inference across the article queue
